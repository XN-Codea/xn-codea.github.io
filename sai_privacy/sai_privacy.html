Your privacy is important. However, SA Institutions app may collect non-identifiable information during browsing session. As it not possible to identify you using this data. This data may include:

2. Usage

SA Institutions app uses certain Google services which makes use of cookies as described in [App Tracking] and other technologies like Google Analytics. The obtained information, specifically non-identifiable information is transferred to Google servers. Google uses this information to track how app is used to provide reports on the App to us and to its advertisers. Google may also transfer this information to third parties if Google is required to do so by law.

3. App Tracking

SA Institutions app may use session cookie [a cookie is a small data file stored on your devices local drive] that only exists until you finish your browser session, these cookies are used to store your data as you move through your browser session but are never accessed by anyone else.

4. Storage & Security

SA Institutions app service is provided by using the hosting service Google. The security of all data is very important and the security of your data will be protected by our trusted service provider "Google".
